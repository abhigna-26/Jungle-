About The Jungle Mist

The Jungle Mist Resort is crafted for those who seek effortless comfort wrapped in nature. Nestled in the hills of Rishikesh, our cottages and cabins blend tasteful luxury with environmental sensitivity. From morning bird songs to star-filled nights around the bonfire, every moment is curated to be memorable.
